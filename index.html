Hi there, I am Kris!

My research focuses on systems for AI and AI for systems. I aim to enable agile hardware and software co-design, facilitating rapid experimentation, cross-stack integration, and data-driven optimization across diverse compute platforms.

My work spans three core directions:

  • Develop infrastructures of design space exploration, and pre-silicon evaluation of domain-specific accelerators,
  • Build unified compiler and runtime for heterogeneous architectures
  • Innovate LLM-based agentic flows that automate code generation and performance optimization.

I am passionate about open-source ecosystems and reproducible research. Two of my main works have been recognized with the Distinguished Artifact Award at ISCA 2023 and the Distinguished Artifact Award at IISWC 2025.
